Coffee first.

Our story begins over 30 years ago, in 1986, when our founders, John Doe and Jane Doe had a vision: a coffee company like no other. Stemming from their passion for the delicious and sumptuous liquor, as well as their philantropy, kaff™ was established. Since then, we have dedicated to making sure everyone can have easy access to carefully crafted, deliciously roasted and sustainably sourced coffee. We view the coffee culture as progressive, and with progression comes the opportunity to innovate. We aim to provide you with an unforgettable experience in every cup, whilst maintaining the same flavor notes and transparent ingredient list. Every detail matters, and therefore we pay attention to even the smallest of aspects in order to make your experience with us top notch.
